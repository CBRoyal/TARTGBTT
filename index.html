How to Book Travel Tickets

International travel is always stressful, especially if you don't have any experience with traveling to different countries. If you're going to certain places that require you to book your own train, ferry, or plane tickets; you need to able to handle them efficiently in-and-out. This webpage will cover all of the booking skills you need to get to your next Amazing Race destination faster and more efficently.

Land Traveling

Now land traveling might be easy if you just need to travel on foot or via taxi to your next Race location, but if it involves complex bus and train traveling to your next faraway Race location, you need to be able to book your bus or train tickets in-and-out properly.To be able to do that, you first need to find the ticketing window and ask for the quickest and earliest bus or train ride to your next Race destination.


Now if there are a limited number of bus or train departures leaving from your current Race destination to your next Race destination, you'll be given tickets for a reserved bus or train departure made by The Amazing Race crew. However, you're under no obligation to use them and you're allow to see if there's an earlier bus or train departure to your next Race destination.

Water Travel

Now when you're visiting a certain metropolitan environment, you might not always have to travel via land, you might need to travel via water. Well that's why they have ferry transportation companies, and to book tickets, you just need to find the ticketing window and ask for the quickest and earliest ferry ride to your next Race location.

Air Travel

Air traveling is the most important aspect of The Amazing Race. In almost every single Amazing Race season, most of the time, you're gonna need to book your own flights to your next Race destination. Now as you probably have seen, air traveling can be complex, but if you follow these guidelines and tips, you'll be able to book your flights to your next Race destination in-and-out.

  • One way to book your flight to your next Race destination is to just go to the airport and ask for the quickest and earliest flight to your next Race destination.

  • Another way to book your flight is to go to the nearest travel agency in your current Race destination and ask for the quickest and earliest flight to your next Race destination.

  • Here's another way to book your flight, try calling the travel agency or the airport and ask for the quickest and earliest flight to your next Race destination.

  • Sometimes you'll be asked to book your own flight and hotel accommodation using the Expedia app.

*Now if there are a limited number of flights leaving from your current Race destination to your next Race destination, you'll be given tickets for a reserved flight made by The Amazing Race crew. However, you're under no obligation to use them and you're allow to see if there's an earlier flight to your next Race destination.

*You're only allowed to book four economy class tickets including tickets for your crew members.
